Welcome to USBPre Auto-Init

This installer will set up automatic initialization for your Sound Devices USBPre audio interface on modern macOS.

What will be installed:

  • Background monitoring daemon
  • Launch configuration (starts at boot)
  • Uninstaller app (in Applications/Utilities)

How it works:

A small background service monitors for your USBPre device. When detected, it automatically sends the initialization sequence that enables full functionality.

Requirements:

  • macOS 11.0 (Big Sur) or later
  • Sound Devices USBPre (VID: 0x0926, PID: 0x0100)
  • Administrator privileges (you'll be prompted for your password)
